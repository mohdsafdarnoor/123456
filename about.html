About the Project

Bridging the gap between international talent and the German job market using AI.

üéØ Our Mission

International students often face significant hurdles:

  • Language Barriers: Professional German is hard.
  • Cultural Norms: German CVs have specific rules.

We aim to democratize career access by providing free, high-quality AI tools and guides acting as a 24/7 career counselor.

This platform is a semester project from the Master International Management program.
