Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use this portal?

This portal is primarily designed for international students at Hochschule Wismar studying International Management, but the resources are useful for any international student in Germany seeking employment.

Are the AI tools free?

Most tools we've listed offer free versions or free trials. Some have premium features that require payment. We've indicated this where applicable in the tool descriptions.

Can I suggest new tools or prompts?

Absolutely! Use the contact form above and select "Suggest a new AI tool" to share your recommendations. We regularly update our content based on student feedback.

Is this an official Hochschule Wismar website?

This is a student project supervised by faculty at Hochschule Wismar. While it's affiliated with the university, always verify critical information with official university departments like the Career Center or International Office.

How often is the content updated?

We review and update our tool listings and prompts quarterly, or more frequently if we discover outdated information or receive important feedback from users.

Can I share this portal with friends at other universities?

Yes! While created for Hochschule Wismar students, the resources are valuable for any international student in Germany. Feel free to share the link with your network.

What should I do if I find incorrect information?

Please report it immediately using the contact form above. Select "Report a technical issue" or "Question about content" and provide details about the error. We'll investigate and correct it as soon as possible.

About This Project

This AI Job Search Support Portal was created as a student project by a team of five International Management Master's students at Hochschule Wismar. Our goal is to help fellow international students navigate the German job market more effectively by curating AI-powered tools and providing ready-made prompts for every stage of the job search process.

We understand the unique challenges faced by international students - from language barriers to unfamiliar application processes - and created this resource to make the journey smoother for everyone.

Disclaimer: This portal is a student-led initiative. While we've carefully researched and curated all content, always verify important information with official sources like your university's Career Service, the Ausländerbehörde (foreigners office), or official government websites.
