Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using AI tools for your job search.

Is this platform free?

Yes, all tools and guides listed are free or have free tiers for students.

Can I use these prompts for any industry?

Yes, the prompts are designed to be adaptable. Just replace the bracketed information with your specific field.

Do I need a paid ChatGPT account?

No, all prompts work with the free version of ChatGPT (GPT-3.5 or GPT-4o mini).
