Is it cheating to use ChatGPT for my cover letter?

No, but use it responsibly. Use AI to structure your thoughts and fix grammar, but always rewrite the final text in your own voice. Recruiters can spot generic AI text.

Are these tools free?

Most tools listed (ChatGPT, Canva, LinkedIn) have free versions sufficient for students. Some like VMock may have paid upgrades.

How many hours can I work?

Non-EU students can work 120 full days or 240 half days per year. During the semester: max 20h/week. Breaks: up to 40h/week.

Mandatory vs. Voluntary Internship?
  • Pflicht (Mandatory): Required by university. No minimum wage, no social security.
  • Freiwillig (Voluntary): Paid minimum wage if >3 months.
