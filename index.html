Important: Use AI Responsibly

These AI tools are designed to assist your job search, not replace your own effort. Many companies automatically reject applications that appear entirely AI-generated.

  • Do: Use AI for drafting, structuring, and improving grammar
  • Do: Personalize all AI-generated content with your real experiences
  • Do: Conduct your own research and fact-checking
  • Don't: Submit AI-generated content without thorough review and editing
  • Don't: Rely entirely on AI for your job search strategy
