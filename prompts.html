Prompt 1: Werkstudent Role - No Experience

Copy this prompt into ChatGPT:

I am an international student looking for a Werkstudent (part-time) role in Germany. Here is my information:

Background Information:
- Degree and field of study: [Your degree, e.g., Master's in International Management]
- University and current semester: [Your university and semester]
- Relevant coursework or academic projects: [List 2-3 relevant courses or projects]
- Tools, software, or methods I'm familiar with: [e.g., Excel, Python, PowerPoint]
- German language level: [A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, or C2]
- English language level: [Your level]
- Weekly availability: [How many hours per week can you work?]

Tasks for ChatGPT:
1. Suggest realistic Werkstudent roles suitable for candidates with no prior work experience
2. Explain which roles are typically open to international students
3. Rewrite my CV to highlight academic strengths and transferable skills
4. Prepare strong answers to the question "Do you have practical experience?"
5. Advise how to position myself as reliable and trainable

I need to create a German-style CV (Lebenslauf) from scratch.

Prompt 2: Write a German Cover Letter (Anschreiben)

I need to write a professional German cover letter.

Background Information:
- Company name and job title: [Where you're applying]
- How I found the job: [Job board, referral, company website]
- Why I'm interested: [Your motivation]
- Key qualifications: [Your relevant experience and skills]
- Start date availability: [When you can start]

Tasks for ChatGPT:
1. Write a professional German Anschreiben following German formatting
2. Address my motivation clearly and authentically
3. Connect my qualifications to the job requirements
4. Use appropriate formal German language
5. Keep it concise (maximum one page)

Prompt 3: Improve Bullet Points for Impact

My CV bullet points are weak and need to show more impact.

Background Information:
- Current bullet points: [Paste 3-5 bullet points from your CV]
- The role or achievement: [What you actually did]

Tasks for ChatGPT:
1. Rewrite each bullet point using action verbs and quantifiable results
2. Show the difference between weak vs. strong bullet points
3. Use the STAR method where appropriate (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
4. Make them ATS-friendly with relevant keywords
5. Keep each bullet point to 1-2 lines maximum
