AI Prompts Library

Copy-paste these prompts into ChatGPT or Claude to accelerate your job search.

Working Student Prompts

CV Rewrite (No Experience)

Context to provide: Degree, semester, coursework, tools known, language level.

Prompt: "Act as a German recruiter. Suggest realistic Werkstudent roles for a student with no prior experience. Then, rewrite my CV to highlight my academic projects and transferable skills. Position me as reliable and trainable."

Cover Letter Email

Context to provide: Role name, company name, key motivation.

Prompt: "Draft a short, professional German email (Anschreiben) to an HR manager expressing interest in the [Role Name] position. Keep it formal, polite, and emphasize my availability and eagerness to learn."

Internship Prompts

Mandatory Internship (Pflichtpraktikum)

Context to provide: Degree, specialization, confirmation it's mandatory.

Prompt: "I am applying for a mandatory internship (Pflichtpraktikum) in Germany. Create a one-page, ATS-friendly German-style CV based on my profile. Translate my coursework into practical skills that a recruiter would value."

Interview Prep (Cultural Fit)

Context to provide: Company type (Startup/Corporate), role.

Prompt: "I have an interview at a German [Company Type]. Help me answer common behavioral questions confidently without overselling myself. Highlight cultural mistakes international students often make."

Rejection Analysis

Context to provide: Paste your CV and the job description.

Prompt: "Analyze my CV against this job description. Identify why a German recruiter might reject my application. Point out unclear elements or missing keywords and suggest concrete improvements."

Master Thesis Prompts

Finding Company Partners

Context to provide: Degree, specialization, preferred industry.

Prompt: "Identify types of companies in Germany (SME vs Corporate) open to Master's thesis collaborations in [Field]. Suggest specific departments to approach and list strategies to find thesis-friendly contacts."

Proposal Email to Company

Context to provide: Topic idea, value to company.

Prompt: "Draft a convincing email to a hiring manager proposing a Master's thesis collaboration. Frame the thesis as a business solution that solves a specific problem for them, not just an academic requirement."

Full-Time Job Prompts

CV Optimization (ATS)

Context to provide: Current CV, target job description.

Prompt: "Rewrite my CV to position me as job-ready for full-time roles in Germany. Optimize it for ATS systems by including relevant keywords from this job description. Use strong action verbs."

Salary Negotiation

Context to provide: Role, location, years of experience.

Prompt: "I have received an offer for [Role] in [City]. The offer is [Amount]. Based on market standards, draft a polite but firm email negotiating for a higher salary or better benefits, explaining my value."
