AI Tools Directory

Select your career stage to see recommended AI tools for orientation, application, interview, and onboarding.

Working Student (Werkstudent)

Orientation (Find Opportunities)
  • LinkedIn Career Explorer: Identify skill gaps and role options.
  • JobMensa: Focused on student jobs in Germany.
  • StepStone: Large database for part-time work.
  • Indeed: Broad search for flexible student roles.
Application
  • Canva: Clean, simple student CV templates.
  • Resume.io: ATS-friendly resume builder.
  • Simplify: Auto-fill applications to save time.
  • Loop CV: Automate job applications.
Interview
  • Google Interview Warmup: Practice basic questions.
  • Interview Lift: Mock interviews for students.
  • Yoodli: Improve speaking confidence.
Onboarding
  • DeepL: Essential for German workplace communication.
  • ChatGPT: Draft emails and clarify tasks.

Internship

Orientation
  • Handshake: Student-focused internship discovery.
  • MeinPraktikum.de: Germany-specific internship portal.
  • LinkedIn Jobs: Filter for "Internship" role type.
  • Coursera: Skill prep for specific internships.
Application
  • VMock: Academic resume feedback and scoring.
  • Jobsgpt: Match CVs to internship descriptions.
  • Canva: Creative portfolios for design roles.
Interview
  • Google Interview Warmup: Entry-level focus.
  • Elevator Pitch App: Perfect your "Tell me about yourself".
Onboarding
  • ChatGPT: Write internship reports and daily summaries.
  • DeepL: Translate technical documentation.

Master Thesis (Company Collaboration)

Orientation
  • Excelerate: Connects students with thesis companies.
  • Studyond: Thesis project matching.
  • Thesisforyou: Platform for thesis topics.
  • LinkedIn: Search "Thesis" in Jobs section.
Application
  • Cheetah AI: Draft thesis proposals and motivation letters.
Interview (Supervisor Meetings)
  • Final Round AI: Structure your research pitch.
  • Interview Lift: Practice defending your topic.
Onboarding (Research Phase)
  • ChatGPT: Literature review assistance and outlining.
  • Coach: Productivity tracking for research goals.
